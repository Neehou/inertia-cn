Team Recognition

We Won! 2026 Best Workplaces™ in Canada

July 2026 Inertia Guangzhou Best Workplace · Dual-City Team · Company Culture

In 2026, we received an especially meaningful gift. We have been officially recognized as a 2026 Best Workplaces™ in Canada. This is not just a title — it is genuine feedback from every day, every effort, and every moment of mutual support across our team.

If this award could speak, it would probably say: Hey, you — that little team spanning the Pacific, bridging mountains and time zones — you've built something remarkable. A workplace with real warmth and genuine quality. Well done.

Part 1 One Award, Across 12 Time Zones

The "Best Workplaces" recognition is based on rigorous, anonymous employee satisfaction surveys and a comprehensive evaluation of company culture, leadership, trust, and sense of belonging. In other words: it's not us saying we're great — it's every colleague, in both cities, saying so.

Great Place To Work® Certified — Canada 2026

Part 2 One Team, Two Cities — A Two-Way Commitment

Our daily work has never been a nine-to-five routine in a single city. It is two cities lighting up in turn — a full-force relay across the Pacific.

Toronto team: Deep expertise in creative design, brand aesthetics, and international market development — connecting frontier resources and delivering brand value. Guangzhou team: Excellence in manufacturing, supply chain orchestration, and delivery services — building product foundations and safeguarding every quality commitment.

Twelve time zones apart. Over ten thousand kilometers of mountains and oceans between us. This is the collaboration challenge woven into our DNA. Time zones shift day into night. Distance makes face-to-face rare. But through every single day and night in the past year, we have never been torn apart by geography.

We have none of the detachment of remote teams. None of the barriers of distributed offices. No "headquarters vs. branch" mentality. No "them" and "us." What we have is the默契 of covering for each other, backing each other up, and passing the baton across day and night — one team, truly aligned, truly together.

Different cities, one heart. Time zones apart, zero distance. The best workplace is never about perfect systems — it's about every person in it feeling they belong, they are safe, and they are valued.

Part 3 This Honor — Built Through Daily Dedication

This recognition is no accident. It is the result of a full year of intentional, day-by-day effort — of looking inward and refining the core of our workplace, one meaningful action at a time.

We insist on respecting every voice. Regardless of role or location, every idea, every suggestion, every piece of feedback is listened to carefully, taken seriously, and responded to — so that every team member has the confidence to speak up.

We champion flexible, efficient ways of working. No rigid office models. No pointless friction. We adapt to cross-timezone, cross-region collaboration rhythms — prioritizing efficiency, making work lighter and more effective.

We continuously provide growth and learning opportunities. Focused on personal development and capability building, we create learning platforms and growth spaces, accompanying every team member as they break through limits and advance — growing together with the team.

We nurture a culture of trust, transparency, and joy. Open communication, full transparency, mutual trust. We strip away unnecessary complexity and preserve what makes work meaningful — professionalism with warmth, excellence with genuine connection.

Management, at its core, operates on three levels: guiding people, orchestrating work, and integrating resources; identifying needs and uniting forces to achieve goals; turning chaos into order, transforming low efficiency into high performance, and empowering individuals to thrive within the whole.

This honor doesn't belong to one city or one group — it belongs to every one of us who spans time zones, holds true to our purpose, and gives it everything we've got. 2026: Rooted in recognition, devoted to our purpose. Onward — across mountains and oceans, building a workplace with ever more warmth, strength, and belonging.
