Amico

Mobile Workstation & Medication Drawers

These mobile medical device workstations are optimized to enhance clinician experience, improve administration efficiency, and deliver better patient outcomes. The ergonomic design includes adjustable monitor height for sitting or standing, tilt and pivot functionality, customizable accessory rails, and oversized casters for lightweight movement. Design also includes cable management, keyboard tray, splash-proof work surface, lockable PC compartment, and medication drawers.

Baylis MEDICAL

100% Oxygen Ventilator Accessory

Baylis engaged Inertia to develop the industrial design for an accessory to the V4C-560 ventilator. Built on Medtronic's PB560 design, the ventilator is small, lightweight, and portable. COVID-19 created demand for higher oxygen saturation — Inertia helped design and manufacture an accessory enabling the V4C-560 to deliver 100% oxygen when needed.

Qvella

Rapid Blood Analyzer

Within minutes, the Qvella blood analyzer can detect 90% of potentially harmful pathogens in blood. It is a transformative product that dramatically reduces the transmission rate of infectious pathogens and accelerates antimicrobial treatment for critically ill patients.

Raz Design

Shower/Toilet Wheelchair

Raz Design and Inertia have partnered since 2014 on 5 mobile shower seats — Zum, Jaz, ART, Rize & Trakz — and Wheelz custom wheelchairs. Comfort, best-in-class seating, and accessibility drive our design approach, with each chair offering options for unique user needs.

Cellview

Ophthalmic Imaging System

Cellview's Non-Mydriatic Ultra-Widefield WRI-1 provides clinicians with an affordable, powerful diagnostic tool for retinal examination. Inertia provided industrial design, engineering, and prototyping compliant with IEC 60601-1 medical device safety standards.

Nuvio

Innovative Mobility Device

Nuvio One features a unique dual-armrest system delivering enhanced stability, mobility, and smooth transitions between sitting, standing, and walking. Inertia also supported Nuvio's product strategy including market research, workshops, user surveys, and field validation.

Nexus Robotics

Autonomous Weeding Robot

Within six months, Inertia completed the full engineering redesign of an autonomous weeding vehicle — covering mechanical structures, body manufacturing, electronics, and control systems. Three months later, they delivered the full suite of parts for the first pilot fleet.

AEVA

Home Hydroponic Garden

They not only designed a beautiful vertical growing garden that blends perfectly into home environments, but also helped us significantly improve gross margins and rapidly scale production to meet growing market demand.
