Mobile Medical Workstation & Medication Drawers

These mobile medical device workstations are optimized to enhance clinician experience, improve administration efficiency, and deliver better patient outcomes. The ergonomic design includes adjustable monitor height for sitting or standing, tilt and pivot functionality, customizable accessory rails, and oversized casters for lightweight movement.

Baylis × Inertia

100% Oxygen Ventilator Accessory

Baylis engaged Inertia to develop the industrial design for an accessory to the V4C-560 ventilator. Built on Medtronic's PB560 design, the ventilator is small, lightweight, and portable. COVID-19 created demand for higher oxygen saturation — Inertia helped design and manufacture an accessory enabling the V4C-560 to deliver 100% oxygen when needed.
