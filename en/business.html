Four Core CapabilitiesFull Spectrum, Concept to Production

Every phase within one system — no handoff chaos, no information loss.

01

Product Design & Engineering

Canadian R&D team translating concepts into manufacturable designs

From initial concept sketches to full engineering documentation, our Toronto team provides end-to-end product design. Industrial design, mechanical engineering, electronics, embedded systems, and human factors — every discipline collaborates within a unified system, ensuring design decisions carry manufacturing context from day one.

Our methodology centers on Design for Manufacturing (DFM): addressing producibility, assembly, and cost constraints during the design phase to avoid downstream rework and budget overruns.

Capabilities: Industrial Design · Mechanical Engineering · Electronics & Embedded · Human Factors · UX/UI · Systems Architecture

02

Prototyping & Validation

Iterate fast and catch every issue before production

Prototypes are not just design verification tools — they are decision-making evidence. Our in-house prototyping facilities cover 3D printing, CNC machining, functional prototype builds, and pilot production validation.

Through rapid iteration cycles, we help clients validate critical assumptions before design freeze. From appearance mockups to functional prototypes, from engineering validation to pilot builds, every step has clear pass/fail criteria.

Capabilities: 3D Printing · CNC Precision Machining · Functional Prototypes · EVT/DVT Validation · Pilot Build Management

03

Medical Device Manufacturing

ISO 13485:2016 certified precision manufacturing

Medical device manufacturing is one of our deepest capabilities. Under ISO 13485:2016 certification, we provide contract manufacturing for Class II and III medical devices. From supplier qualification and incoming inspection through process validation (IQ/OQ/PQ) to final product verification, every step operates under rigorous international standards.

Our engineering team brings cross-disciplinary expertise spanning precision fluid sensing, electrochemical diagnostics, surgical monitoring, and mobile medical workstations.

Capabilities: ISO 13485:2016 Certified · IQ/OQ/PQ Validation · Supplier Qualification · Cleanroom Assembly · Traceability Systems

04

Global Supply Chain Management

Dual-shore supply chain, single-point accountability

Supply chain management is fundamentally about risk management. We provide dual-shore supply chain solutions for both Chinese and North American clients — from supplier sourcing and qualification through procurement execution, quality control, and logistics delivery, with full transparency and control.

China Go-Global: We help Chinese hardware companies navigate international engineering and regulatory requirements — from design optimization and compliance certification to overseas manufacturing partnerships.

NA Supply Chain Access: Our China-based engineering and quality team provides localized supplier management, quality auditing, and production oversight for North American clients — reducing cross-border communication costs and quality risks.

Capabilities: Supplier Sourcing & Audit · Procurement Execution · IQC/IPQC/OQC · Logistics & Customs · Dual-Shore Project Management
